Welcome to A.R.D

Are you looking for a friendly boxing and kickboxing gym?

A.R.D. Training Camp was first established in 1993, named after it's founder- Andre Robert Daltrey, and has produced well over 100 Champions in boxing and kickboxing.

We have a number of coaches and instructors who have fought at the highest levels and have been involved in boxing and kickboxing for many, many years.

We are well known for our professionalism and care for each of our members and fighters. Although we are only a small gym, we have a big community and have produced many big success stories throughout our 26 years as a gym!

Above all, A.R.D. Training Camp is a place where you'll learn, have fun and be a part of a positive community - meeting people who are dedicated to helping you get the most out of your training and your experiences in the gym.

Our Coaches

All of the coaches listed below are available for 1-to-1 training and all hold their own classes here at A.R.D. Training Camp - you can click on their names to learn more about them and their accolades in the sport!

Andre Daltrey: Owner

Andre is the gym owner and head trainer at A.R.D. Training Camp. He is a licensed Pro Boxing Trainer by the BBBoC (British Boxing Board of Control) and won the WSKA Full Contact Kickboxing World Cruiserweight title in 2000. So he knows a bit! On top of that, he sepnt two years training and fighting in Thailand and has been involved in the training camps of: Manny Pacquiao, Floyd Mayweather,Ricky Hatton and Amir Khan.

Steve Fox

Steve guided Andre to his World title victory in 2000. As an Amateur Boxer, Steve had over 200 fights and he boxed as a Professional as well; so has a vast amount of knowledge and first hand experience in the sport. Steve has assisted Andre in training over 100 Champions and is a BBBoC licensed Pro Boxing Trainer.

Tyler White

Tyler is an ABA Qualified Boxing Coach and he's been training at A.R.D. since the age of 13! He's also won a few things: Two silver medals at the WAKO British Championships, Silver in the WFMC World Championships, former IKF English Kickboxing Champion, as well as Boxing titles and he's passed the Elite Fighters Selection Course.
